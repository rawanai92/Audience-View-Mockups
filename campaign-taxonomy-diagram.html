Key distinction — Category vs Channel
advertisingChannel = CRM exists in both CorePilot and DSP — but they are different things. In CorePilot it means "communication via CRM channels". In DSP it means "ad serving via CRM targeting with a budget and serving engine."

The fix: Add a campaignCategory: Communication | Ad field to the campaign model. The app context sets this at creation time — CorePilot always creates Communication, DSP always creates Ad. No user selection. No enum changes to AdvertisingChannels.
